Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about my services and process

What services do you offer?

I offer full-stack web development, mobile app development, UI/UX design, and consulting services. I specialize in React, Vue.js, Node.js, and modern web technologies.

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary depending on complexity and requirements. A simple website might take 2-4 weeks, while a complex web application could take 2-6 months. I provide detailed timelines during our initial consultation.

Do you work with clients remotely?

Yes, I work with clients worldwide remotely. I use modern collaboration tools and maintain regular communication throughout the project to ensure everything runs smoothly.

What's your development process?

My process includes: 1) Discovery & Planning, 2) Design & Prototyping, 3) Development & Testing, 4) Deployment & Launch, 5) Maintenance & Support. I keep clients involved at every step.
